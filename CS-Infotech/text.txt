Artificial intelligence is transforming the way people work, learn, and solve problems. From recommending movies to helping doctors diagnose diseases, AI has become a part of everyday life. Learning Python is an excellent starting point for anyone interested in data science and machine learning because it offers simple syntax and powerful libraries. Consistent practice is more important than memorizing code, as writing programs regularly helps build confidence and problem-solving skills. Every mistake made while coding is an opportunity to understand concepts more deeply and improve as a programmer.
